French Onion Soup

French onion soup served with a piece of baguette and gooey cheese

This delicious soup takes around 80 minutes to prepare and it is not too difficult to make.

Ingredients

  • 6 onions, peeled, and thinly sliced
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Kosher salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 8 cups beef stock or chicken stock, or combination
  • 1/2 dry white wine
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 8 slices of baguette
  • 1/2 cups grated Gruyere
  • Sprinkling of parmesan

Cooking Instructions

  1. Carmelize onions for 15-20 minutes
  2. Deglaze pot with wine
  3. Add stock, bay leaves and thyme and cook for 30 minutes
  4. Toast baguettes with grated Gruyere and parmesan
  5. Serve soup with a cheesy baguette
