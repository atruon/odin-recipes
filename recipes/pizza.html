Delicious Margherita Pizza Recipe

A savoury margherita pizza with plenty of cheese and basil on top

This magherita pizza shines with its fresh mozarella and tomatoes. It is not only tasty, but it is easy to make.

40 minutes is all that is needed to cook up this pizza. The instructions can be found below.

Ingredients

  • pizza crust/dough
  • 1x 14-oz can of whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 ground black peppers
  • 2-3 Roma tomatoes
  • 7-8 oz of fresh mozarella
  • 1 oz fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Drain and puree tomatoes in a processor
  3. Spread sauce over the dough and add the slices of mozarella on top
  4. Bake pizza for 8-10 minutes
  5. Add fresh basil, let sit for 5 minutes, slice and serve
